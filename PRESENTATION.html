Four Core Leadership Dimensions

1. Transparency

Clear communication, admitting mistakes, encouraging open dialogue

Critical for tech, finance, healthcare sectors

2. Moral/Ethical

Values-based decisions, ethical conduct, consistency

Essential across all industries

3. Balanced Processing

Data-driven decisions, soliciting diverse views, analytical thinking

Key for consulting, analytics, engineering

4. Self-Awareness

Understanding impact, seeking feedback, accurate self-assessment
